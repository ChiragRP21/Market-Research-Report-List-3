The LDPE market can be segmented into different types based on the comonomer used during the production process. These types include C4, C6, and C8, which refer to the carbon chain length of the comonomer. Each type has unique properties and applications, with C4 being used for film production, C6 for pipe coatings, and C8 for adhesive formulations, contributing to the diverse range of LDPE products available in the market.</p></p>
