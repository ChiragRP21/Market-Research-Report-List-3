<p><p>Antiblocking agents are additives used in the manufacturing of plastic films to prevent them from sticking together. There are two main types of antiblocking agents available in the market: organic and inorganic. Organic antiblocking agents are derived from natural sources such as plant waxes or animal fats, while inorganic antiblocking agents are typically synthetic compounds like silica or talc. Both types of antiblocking agents serve the same purpose of improving the handling and storage of plastic films by reducing adhesion between layers.</p></p>
