<p><p>EV Battery Thermal Management System plays a crucial role in maintaining optimal temperature for efficient performance and longevity of batteries. Active Management Systems use cooling and heating mechanisms to regulate temperature actively, ensuring consistent performance. Passive Management Systems rely on natural cooling methods like air circulation or phase-change materials to maintain temperature. While active systems offer precise control, passive systems are cost-effective and require less maintenance. Both types are essential for ensuring the safety and efficiency of EV batteries.</p></p>
