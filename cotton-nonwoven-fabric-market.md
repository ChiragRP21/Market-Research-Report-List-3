<p><p>Cotton nonwoven fabric market can be categorized into two main types: cross layer type and parallel type. Cross layer type involves layers of cotton fibers that are laid perpendicular to each other, creating a strong and durable fabric. On the other hand, parallel type consists of cotton fibers that are aligned in the same direction, providing a smooth and even surface. Both types offer unique characteristics and cater to different applications in industries such as textiles, healthcare, and construction.</p></p>
