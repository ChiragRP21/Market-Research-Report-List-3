<p><ul><li>IT system</li><li>TN-S system</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Isolated Hospital Power System market includes two main types: IT system and TN-S system. IT system refers to a setup where the neutral point of the transformer is isolated from the ground, providing increased protection against electric shock and equipment damage. TN-S system, on the other hand, involves a combined protective conductor and neutral conductor, offering a simpler and more cost-effective solution for hospitals. Both systems are crucial for ensuring reliable and safe power supply in healthcare facilities.</p></p>
