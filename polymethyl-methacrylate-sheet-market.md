<p><p>Polymethyl methacrylate (PMMA) sheets are available in two main types - extruded and cast. Extruded PMMA sheets are produced by pushing molten plastic through a die, resulting in a more consistent thickness and lower cost. Cast PMMA sheets, on the other hand, are manufactured by pouring liquid plastic into a mold and allowing it to cool and harden, producing a higher quality, optically clearer product. Both types are widely used in various industries due to their versatility, durability, and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
