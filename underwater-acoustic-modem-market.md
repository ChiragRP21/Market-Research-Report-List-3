<p><strong>The Underwater Acoustic Modem Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shallow Water (Up to 350 Meters)</li><li>Medium Range (Up to 1500 Meters)</li><li>Long Range (Up to 6000 Meters)</li><li>Full Ocean Range (Up to 10000 Meters)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Underwater acoustic modems are categorized based on their operating ranges. Shallow water modems operate up to 350 meters, making them suitable for coastal monitoring and communication. Medium-range modems can reach depths of up to 1500 meters, ideal for offshore activities. Long-range modems can transmit data up to 6000 meters deep, suitable for deep-sea exploration. Full ocean range modems have the capability to operate at depths of up to 10000 meters, making them suitable for deep-ocean research and underwater observatories.</p></p>
