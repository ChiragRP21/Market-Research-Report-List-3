<p><p>Queue kiosks come in three market types: floor-standing, wall-mounted, and countertop. Floor-standing kiosks are typically used in larger spaces with high traffic areas, providing a convenient way for customers to join a queue. Wall-mounted kiosks are ideal for areas with limited floor space, such as corridors or waiting rooms. Countertop kiosks are suitable for smaller businesses or limited space environments where a more compact queue management system is needed. Each type offers a different solution for managing queues efficiently and effectively.</p></p>
