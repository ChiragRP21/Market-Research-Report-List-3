<p><p>Inhalation Therapy Nebuliser Market consists of three main types: Pneumatic Nebulisers, Ultrasonic Nebulisers, and Mesh Nebulisers. Pneumatic Nebulisers use compressed air to convert liquid medication into a mist for inhalation. Ultrasonic Nebulisers use ultrasonic waves to create a fine mist, while Mesh Nebulisers use a vibrating mesh to generate aerosol particles. Each type of nebuliser has its own advantages and limitations, catering to different patient needs and preferences in the treatment of respiratory conditions.</p></p>
