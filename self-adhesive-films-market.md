<p><p>Self-adhesive films come in three main types based on their appearance: translucent, transparent, and opaque. Translucent films allow some light to pass through, creating a soft glow and adding privacy. Transparent films let in maximum light and provide a clear view. Opaque films completely block light and visibility, offering privacy and obstruction of views. These different types cater to various needs and preferences, providing versatile options for applications such as window glass coverings, signage, decoration, and branding.</p></p>
