<p><p>Water-based adhesives can be categorized into two main types: natural and synthetic. Natural water-based adhesives are made from renewable resources such as starch, protein, or plant-based materials. They are environmentally friendly and have low toxicity levels. On the other hand, synthetic water-based adhesives are manufactured from petroleum-based chemicals. They are known for their strong boning capabilities and resistance to moisture. Both types of adhesives have their own unique characteristics and applications in various industries.</p></p>
