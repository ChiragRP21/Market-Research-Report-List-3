<p><ul><li>Scalable Video Coding (SVC) Type</li><li>Advanced Video Coding (AVC) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cloud Videoconferencing Solution market types refer to different technologies used in videoconferencing software. Scalable Video Coding (SVC) Type utilizes a flexible compression technique that adapts to network conditions, allowing for better video quality and less data consumption. On the other hand, Advanced Video Coding (AVC) Type uses a standardized compression method, providing good video quality but with higher data requirements. Both types cater to different needs, with SVC offering scalability and adaptability, while AVC focuses on maintaining high-quality video.</p></p>
