<p><p>Highly-elastic coupling market can be divided into two main types - Spiral Groove Type and Parallel Groove Type. Spiral Groove Type couplings utilize a helical groove design to provide flexibility and accommodate misalignment. On the other hand, Parallel Groove Type couplings use a parallel groove design to achieve the same benefits. Both types offer high elasticity and can withstand high levels of torque and shock loads, making them suitable for applications where flexibility and durability are key requirements.</p></p>
