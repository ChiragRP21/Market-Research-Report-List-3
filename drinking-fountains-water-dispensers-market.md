<p><p>Drinking fountains, also known as water dispensers, come in two main types: Bottled Water Dispenser or Bottled Water Cooler (BWC) and Point-of-Use (POU) market. Bottled water dispensers use large water bottles that are placed on top of the unit, while POU dispensers are connected directly to a water source. BWC systems are convenient for areas without access to plumbing, while POU systems provide a more sustainable and cost-effective option for locations with regular access to clean water.</p></p>
