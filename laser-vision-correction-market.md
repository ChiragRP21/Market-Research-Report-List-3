<p><p>Laser Vision Correction is a medical procedure used to correct vision problems such as nearsightedness, farsightedness, and astigmatism. There are two main types of lasers used in this market: Excimer Laser and Femtosecond Laser. The Excimer Laser is used to reshape the cornea, allowing light to properly focus on the retina. On the other hand, the Femtosecond Laser is used to create a thin corneal flap, which is then lifted to perform the necessary corneal reshaping. Both lasers play a crucial role in providing patients with improved vision and eliminating the need for glasses or contact lenses.</p></p>
