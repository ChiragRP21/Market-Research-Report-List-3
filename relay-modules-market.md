<p><p>Relay modules are available in various types in the market. Switching relay modules are used for controlling high-power electrical circuits. Electromechanical relay modules use electromagnets to mechanically switch circuits on and off. DC relay modules are specifically designed to work with direct current circuits. Solid-state relay modules use semiconductor devices to perform switching functions. Other market types may include specialty relay modules for specific applications such as time-delay relays or reed relays. Each type of relay module offers unique features and advantages for different electrical systems.</p></p>
