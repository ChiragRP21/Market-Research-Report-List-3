<p><p>Zinc Oxide Active Market Types include Direct Process (America Method), Indirect Process (French Method), and Wet Chemical Process. In the Direct Process, zinc metal is melted and vaporized, then oxidized by air to form zinc oxide. The Indirect Process involves the vaporization of zinc metal by burning it in air, followed by oxidation to produce zinc oxide. The Wet Chemical Process involves the reaction of zinc salts with alkali to form zinc hydroxide, which is then calcined to produce zinc oxide.</p></p>
