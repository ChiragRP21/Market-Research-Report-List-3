<p><p>Water-Based Ink market types can be classified based on the type of resin used in the formulation. The main types of resin used in water-based inks are Acrylic Resin, Polyester Resin, Maleic Resin, and Others Resin. Acrylic Resin provides good adhesion and flexibility, Polyester Resin offers high durability and chemical resistance, Maleic Resin gives improved pigment dispersion and ink gloss, while Other Resins include various types like cellulose, polyurethane, and epoxy resins. Each type of resin brings different properties and benefits to water-based ink formulations.</p></p>
