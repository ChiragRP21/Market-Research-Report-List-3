<p><p>The tele-operated marine drone market can be categorized into two types - monohull and catamaran. Monohull drones have a single, streamlined hull design which makes them suitable for high-speed operations and rough waters. On the other hand, catamaran drones have two hulls connected by a deck, providing stability and a larger working platform for various marine tasks. Both types offer unique advantages depending on the specific requirements of the operation, such as speed, stability, payload capacity, and maneuverability in different marine environments.</p></p>
