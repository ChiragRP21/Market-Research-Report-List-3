<p><p>Flexible cloth-based sensors are becoming increasingly popular due to their versatility and ease of integration into various applications. Two key types of sensors in this market are pressure sensors and tension sensors. Pressure sensors measure the force applied to a surface, making them useful in applications like wearable health monitors or touch-sensitive devices. Tension sensors, on the other hand, measure the level of strain or tension in a material, which can be valuable in applications like monitoring fabric tension in textile manufacturing or ensuring proper fit in wearable devices.</p></p>
