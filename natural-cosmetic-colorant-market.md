<p><p>Natural cosmetic colorants are classified into three main types based on their source: plant colorants, animal colorants, and microbial colorants. Plant colorants are derived from fruits, vegetables, flowers, and other plant materials. Animal colorants are obtained from sources such as insects, shellfish, and carmine. Microbial colorants are produced through fermentation of microorganisms such as bacteria and fungi. Each type of colorant provides unique shades and properties, catering to the growing demand for natural and sustainable cosmetic products.</p></p>
