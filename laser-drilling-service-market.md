<p><p>The Laser Drilling Service market encompasses various types of drilling techniques. Percussion Laser Drilling involves the use of repetitive pulsing to create holes with minimal heat accumulation. Trephine Laser Drilling involves the removal of a cylindrical core material from the workpiece using laser beams. Other drilling techniques include methods such as percussion trepanning, which combines the characteristics of both percussion and trephine drilling. These techniques are commonly used in industries like aerospace, automotive, and electronics for precise hole drilling in materials like metals, ceramics, and composites.</p></p>
