<p><p>Noise barriers in the market can be divided into three main types: reflective, absorptive, and mixed. Reflective barriers are designed to deflect noise away from a certain area, while absorptive barriers are designed to absorb noise and reduce its impact. Mixed barriers combine elements of both reflective and absorptive types to provide a comprehensive noise reduction solution. Each type of barrier has its own advantages and disadvantages, making it important for customers to choose the best option based on their specific noise control needs.</p></p>
