<p><p>Mould release oil is available in two main types: water-based and oil-based. It is ideal for use in industries where water contamination is a concern. Oil-based mould release oil, on the other hand, is made using oil as the base solvent, providing a more durable and long-lasting option. It is commonly used in industries where heavy-duty mould release is required.</p></p>
