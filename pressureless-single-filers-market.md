<p><strong>The Pressureless Single Filers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OpenTop</li><li>ClosedTop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressureless single filers can be classified into two main market types: OpenTop and ClosedTop. OpenTop single filers allow for easy access to the product being conveyed and are typically used when no containment of the product is required. On the other hand, ClosedTop single filers have enclosed sides and are designed to contain the product as it moves down the line. The choice between OpenTop and ClosedTop depends on the specific needs of the application, such as product fragility or contamination concerns.</p></p>
