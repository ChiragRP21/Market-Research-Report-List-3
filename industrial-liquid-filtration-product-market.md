<p><p>Industrial liquid filtration products are essential in various industries for separating solid particles from liquids. The main types of liquid filtration products include filter press, bag filter, drum filter, depth filter, and others. Filter press uses pressure to separate particles, while bag filter uses a fabric bag to trap particles. Drum filter operates using rotational filtration, and depth filter uses depth filtration methods for particle separation. Other types may include cartridge filters, membrane filters, and mesh filters among others. Each type has specific applications and benefits in industrial liquid filtration processes.</p></p>
