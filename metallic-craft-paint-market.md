<p><p>Metallic craft paint is available in two main types: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based metallic craft paint contains chemicals that help it adhere well to surfaces and dry quickly, making it ideal for outdoor projects or surfaces that may be exposed to water or harsh conditions. Water-based metallic craft paint, on the other hand, is more environmentally friendly and easier to clean up with water. It is suitable for indoor projects and is popular among consumers looking for a safer alternative to solvent-based paints.</p></p>
