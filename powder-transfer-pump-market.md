<p><p>Powder transfer pumps are categorized into three main types based on their power source: pneumatic, electric, and others. Pneumatic powder transfer pumps utilize compressed air to move powders, making them suitable for explosive environments. Electric powder transfer pumps are powered by electricity, offering a more consistent and controlled transfer process. The other category includes powder transfer pumps that operate using different power sources or mechanisms such as hydraulic or manual pumps. Each type has its own advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
