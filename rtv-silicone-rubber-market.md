<p><strong>The RTV Silicone Rubber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RTV-1</li><li>RTV-2</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RTV silicone rubber is categorized into two main types - RTV-1 and RTV-2. RTV-1 refers to one-component silicone rubber, which is ready to use and requires no mixing before application. On the other hand, RTV-2 refers to two-component silicone rubber, which requires mixing of base and catalyst before use. RTV-1 is commonly used for simple applications, while RTV-2 is preferred for more complex projects that require customization and precise control over curing time.</p></p>
