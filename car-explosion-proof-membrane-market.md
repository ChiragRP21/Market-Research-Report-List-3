<p><p>Car explosion-proof membrane market offers two main types of films: heat absorption film and reflective insulation film. Heat absorption film works by absorbing and dissipating heat, providing protection against explosions and high temperatures. Reflective insulation film, on the other hand, reflects heat away from the vehicle, reducing the risk of explosions caused by overheating. Both types of films serve the common purpose of enhancing car safety by providing an additional layer of protection against potential dangers like explosions.</p></p>
