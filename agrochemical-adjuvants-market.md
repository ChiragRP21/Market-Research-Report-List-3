<p><ul><li>Alkoxylates</li><li>Sulfonates</li><li>Silicone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Agrochemical adjuvants play a crucial role in enhancing the effectiveness of agricultural chemicals. Three common types of adjuvants in the market are Alkoxylates, Sulfonates, and Silicones. Alkoxylates are known for their ability to improve spreading and wetting of chemicals on plant surfaces. Sulfonates are effective at enhancing the absorption and penetration of chemicals into plant tissues. Silicones are valued for their ability to reduce surface tension, resulting in improved coverage and adherence of chemicals on plants. These adjuvants help optimize the performance of agrochemicals in agriculture.</p></p>
