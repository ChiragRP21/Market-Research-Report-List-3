<p><ul><li>Flow-through Carrier</li><li>Wall-flow Carrier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Honeycomb ceramic catalyst carriers are utilized in pollution control systems in various industries. There are two main types of carriers in the market: flow-through and wall-flow carriers. Flow-through carriers allow the exhaust gases to pass through the channels, providing effective contact between the catalyst and the gases. On the other hand, wall-flow carriers force the gases to flow through the porous walls of the carrier, allowing for better filtration of pollutants. Both types play a crucial role in reducing emissions and improving air quality.</p></p>
