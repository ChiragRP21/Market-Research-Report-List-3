<p><p>Hemoglobin A1c Monitoring Market includes various types of methods such as Ion-exchange, High-performance liquid chromatography (HPLC), Immunoassay, Boronate affinity chromatography, and Direct enzymatic assay. Ion-exchange method separates hemoglobin variants based on charge, while HPLC separates them based on size. Immunoassay uses antibodies to detect A1c levels, Boronate affinity chromatography utilizes boronate columns for separation, and Direct enzymatic assay directly measures A1c levels using enzymes. These methods offer different approaches to accurately monitor and measure Hemoglobin A1c levels.</p></p>
