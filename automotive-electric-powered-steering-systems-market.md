<p><p>Automotive Electric Powered Steering Systems market offers various types of steering systems, including Column Type, Pinion-and-rack Type, Double Pinion Type, and Ball screw Type. Column Type steering systems feature a shaft connected to the steering wheel. Pinion-and-rack Type systems utilize a pinion gear on the steering shaft to turn a rack and pinion gear mechanism. Double Pinion Type systems involve two pinion gears working together for precise steering control. Ball screw Type systems use a ball screw mechanism for smooth and efficient steering operation.</p></p>
