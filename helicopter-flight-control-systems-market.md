<p><p>Helicopter Flight Control Systems market consists of two main types: Primary Flight Control System and Secondary Flight Control System. The Primary Flight Control System is responsible for controlling the helicopter's attitude and airspeed, including the main rotor and tail rotor controls. On the other hand, the Secondary Flight Control System assists in controlling the helicopter's stability, maneuverability, and navigation, such as the trim system and autopilot. Both systems work together to ensure safe and efficient helicopter operations.</p></p>
