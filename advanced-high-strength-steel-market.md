<p><p>Advanced high-strength steel market types include Dual Phase (DP), Transformation-induced Plasticity (TRIP), Complex Phase (CP), and others. DP steel offers a combination of strength and ductility, making it suitable for structural components. TRIP steel undergoes a phase transformation process, enhancing its formability and strength. CP steel provides high strength and formability, ideal for automotive applications. Other types of advanced high-strength steel may combine various elements to offer unique properties for specific applications in industries such as automotive and construction.</p></p>
