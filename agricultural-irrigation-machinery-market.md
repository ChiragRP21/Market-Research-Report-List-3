<p><p>Agricultural irrigation machinery market consists of different types of irrigation systems such as sprinkler irrigation and drip irrigation. Sprinkler irrigation systems distribute water through a network of pipes and sprinkler heads, resembling rainfall. This system is suitable for a variety of crops and soil types. Drip irrigation, on the other hand, delivers water directly to the root zone of plants through a network of tubing and emitters. This method is more water-efficient and reduces water wastage, making it ideal for areas with limited water resources.</p></p>
