These different types cater to various industrial applications, each offering unique properties and characteristics. Polycarbonate Diols with a molecular weight of <1000 G/Mol are typically used for coatings and adhesives, while those in the 1000-2000 G/Mol range are suitable for sealants and elastomers. Polycarbonate Diols with a molecular weight of >2000 G/Mol are ideal for producing high-performance polyurethanes and thermoplastic elastomers.</p></p>
