<p><p>Embedded touch displays are categorized based on the type of technology used in the display. LCD technology, or Liquid Crystal Display, uses a backlight to illuminate pixels. LED technology, or Light Emitting Diode, uses LEDs for backlighting, offering better contrast and energy efficiency. OLED technology, or Organic Light Emitting Diode, uses organic compounds for self-illumination, providing deeper blacks and higher contrast. Other technologies in this market include E-paper displays and resistive touch panels, each offering unique advantages for specific applications.</p></p>
