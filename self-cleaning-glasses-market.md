<p><p>The self-cleaning glasses market includes two main types of coatings: hydrophobic and hydrophilic. Hydrophobic coatings repel water and other liquids, preventing dirt and grime from sticking to the surface. On the other hand, hydrophilic coatings attract water, causing it to spread out and wash away debris more easily. Both types of coatings offer benefits in terms of maintaining cleanliness and reducing the need for regular cleaning of the glasses.</p></p>
