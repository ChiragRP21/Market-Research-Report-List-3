<p><p>The Transport Management System (TMS) market can be categorized into different types. Transport sourcing involves finding the best transport providers for specific needs. Capacity management focuses on optimizing the utilization of available transport resources. Performance management tracks and assesses the efficiency of transport operations. Network design entails planning the most effective transport network structure. Shipping consolidation combines smaller shipments into larger ones for cost savings. Route planning aims to identify the most efficient routes for transportation. These types of services and solutions are offered in the TMS market to enhance transportation efficiency and optimize logistics operations.</p></p>
