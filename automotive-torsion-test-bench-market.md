<p><p>The Automotive Torsion Test Bench Market includes different types such as Yield Intensity, Torsional Rigidity, Shear Stress, and Ductility. Yield Intensity refers to the point at which a material begins to deform plastically. Torsional Rigidity measures a material's resistance to twisting. Shear Stress is the force parallel to a material's surface that causes deformation. Ductility is a material's ability to undergo plastic deformation before breaking. These market types help automotive manufacturers understand the performance and durability of materials used in vehicle components.</p></p>
