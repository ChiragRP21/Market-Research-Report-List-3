<p><p>HPLC (High-Performance Liquid Chromatography) chips come in two main types - Single Phase and Three Phase. Single Phase chips have a simpler design and are generally used for routine or less complex separations. Three Phase chips, on the other hand, have an additional phase for enhanced separation efficiency and are often used for more complex separations or analyses. Both types offer high performance and efficiency in separating and analyzing various compounds in a liquid sample.</p></p>
