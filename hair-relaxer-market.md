<p><p>Hair relaxer market is divided into two main types: lye relaxer and no lye relaxer. Lye relaxers are made with sodium hydroxide and are known for their strong straightening capabilities. They are harsher on the hair and can cause more damage if not used properly. On the other hand, no lye relaxers use calcium hydroxide or guanidine hydroxide as their active ingredient. They are milder and gentler on the hair, making them a popular choice for those with sensitive scalps or damaged hair.</p></p>
