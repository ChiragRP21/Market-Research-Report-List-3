<p><ul><li>AGM</li><li>EFB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TAXI battery market consists of two main types: Absorbent Glass Mat (AGM) and Enhanced Flooded Battery (EFB). AGM batteries are known for their high-performance capabilities, as they have a high charge acceptance and discharge rate. They are also maintenance-free and provide strong power backup for taxis. On the other hand, EFB batteries offer improved cycling capabilities and increased longevity compared to conventional batteries. They are built to handle the frequent stop-and-start driving conditions of taxis, making them more efficient and cost-effective in the long run.</p></p>
