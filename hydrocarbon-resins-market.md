<p><strong>The Hydrocarbon Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C5, Aliphatic Resins</li><li>C9, Aromatic Resins</li><li>C5/C9, Aliphatic/Aromatic Resins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydrocarbon resins are categorized into four main types based on their composition and properties. C5 resins are derived from piperylene and isoprene, while C9 resins are produced from aromatic monomers. Aromatic resins are extracted from petroleum distillates, and aliphatic resins are made from aliphatic hydrocarbons. C5/C9 resins combine the characteristics of both C5 and C9 resins, while aliphatic/aromatic resins blend the properties of aliphatic and aromatic resins. These different types cater to various industrial applications based on their specific qualities.</p></p>
