<p><p>Coolant reservoir hoses play a crucial role in the proper functioning of a vehicle's cooling system. There are two main types of coolant reservoir hoses on the market - upper and lower hoses. The upper hose connects the top of the radiator to the engine's thermostat housing, while the lower hose connects the bottom of the radiator to the water pump. Both hoses are essential for circulating coolant from the reservoir to the engine to regulate its temperature effectively and ensure optimal performance.</p></p>
