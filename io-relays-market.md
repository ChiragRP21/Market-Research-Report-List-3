<p><p>I/O relays can be classified into two main types: electromechanical relays and solid-state relays. Electromechanical relays use electromagnetic coils to open or close circuits, providing a reliable and durable solution for industrial applications. On the other hand, solid-state relays use semiconductors to switch signals, offering faster response times and longer lifespan. Both types of relays are commonly used in industrial automation, process control, and power distribution systems to control the flow of current and signals within a circuit.</p></p>
