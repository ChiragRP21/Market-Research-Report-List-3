<p><p>5G technology in emergency services has three main market types: Enhanced Mobile Broadband (EMBB), Ultra-Reliable Low-Latency Communications (URLLC), and Massive Machine Type Communications (MMTC). EMBB enables high-speed data transmission for tasks such as sending large files or videos during emergencies. URLLC ensures reliable and low-latency communication crucial for real-time operations like remote surgery or autonomous vehicles. MMTC is designed for connecting a massive number of IoT devices to improve situational awareness and response coordination in emergencies.</p></p>
