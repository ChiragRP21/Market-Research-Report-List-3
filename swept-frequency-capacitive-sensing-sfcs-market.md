<p><p>Swept Frequency Capacitive Sensing (SFCS) technology is used in various markets such as surface capacitance, projected capacitance, self-capacitance, and mutual capacitance. Surface capacitance measures changes in capacitance on a surface, while projected capacitance detects touch through a conductive material. Self-capacitance measures the capacitance of a single object, while mutual capacitance detects changes between two objects. These different market types utilize SFCS technology to provide precise and reliable touch sensing solutions for a wide range of applications.</p></p>
