<p><p>CDU (Control Display Unit) and MCDU (Multifunction Control Display Unit) are types of Aircraft Flight Management Systems. CDU is a manual input device that allows pilots to input data such as flight plans and navigation information, while MCDU is a more advanced version that integrates multiple functions into one unit. MCDU also includes features like automatic data entry, voice recognition, and touchscreen capabilities. Both CDU and MCDU play a crucial role in ensuring the efficient operation of modern aircraft systems.</p></p>
