<p><strong>The White Foam Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Cell</li><li>Open Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>White foam glass can be classified into two main types based on cell structure: closed cell and open cell. Closed cell foam glass has sealed cells that do not connect with each other, providing excellent thermal insulation and resistance to moisture penetration. Open cell foam glass, on the other hand, has interconnected cells that allow for more flexibility and water absorption. Both types have their unique advantages and applications in the construction industry, offering different solutions for insulation needs.</p></p>
