<p><ul><li>Thermally Cured</li><li>Pressure Cured</li><li>Others (Chemically Cured and Light cured)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bonding films market can be categorized into three types based on curing methods: thermally cured, pressure cured, and others such as chemically cured and light cured. Thermally cured bonding films require heat to cure the adhesive, pressure cured films require mechanical pressure for curing, and chemically cured films use a chemical reaction for curing. Light cured films use ultraviolet light for curing. Each type offers unique benefits and is used in various industries depending on the specific bonding requirements.</p></p>
