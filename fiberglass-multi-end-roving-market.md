<p><p>Fiberglass Multi End Roving is primarily divided into two main market types: Continuous Multi End Roving and Chopped Multi End Roving. Continuous Multi End Roving is typically used in applications where a continuous length of roving is required, such as filament winding and pultrusion. On the other hand, Chopped Multi End Roving is used in applications where shorter chopped lengths of roving are needed, such as in spray-up and molding processes. Each market type serves specific purposes in the fiberglass industry, catering to different manufacturing needs.</p></p>
