<p><strong>The Inline pH Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Combination pH Sensors</li><li>Differential pH Sensors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inline pH sensors are available in various types to suit different applications. Combination pH sensors combine both pH and reference electrodes in one unit for convenient measurement. Differential pH sensors use two electrodes to measure the potential difference between them, providing more accurate readings by eliminating interferences. Other types of inline pH sensors include solid-state sensors and encapsulated sensors, which offer durability and longevity in harsh environments. Each type of sensor has its own advantages and is used in specific industries based on their unique features and capabilities.</p></p>
