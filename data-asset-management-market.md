<p><p>Data Asset Management (DAM) market types refer to different deployment options available for managing data assets within organizations. Cloud-based DAM involves storing and managing data assets on remote servers accessed over the internet. It offers scalability and flexibility but may pose security concerns. Conversely, on-premise DAM involves hosting data assets locally within an organization's infrastructure, providing better control and security but requiring significant upfront investments and maintenance. These options provide organizations the choice to decide where and how they want to manage their data assets based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
