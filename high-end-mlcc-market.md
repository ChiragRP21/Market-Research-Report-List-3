<p><p>High-end MLCCs are categorized based on the type of dielectric material used in their construction. X7R MLCCs are known for their stable capacitance over a wide temperature range. X5R MLCCs offer a higher capacitance and more stable performance at lower voltages. C0G MLCCs, also known as NP0, provide the most stable and reliable performance with minimal capacitance change over temperature and voltage. Other types of high-end MLCCs include those with specialty materials such as Barium Titanate or those designed for specific applications like automotive or aerospace.</p></p>
