<p><p>The high-end premium tequila market can be categorized into different types based on their aging process and characteristics. Blanco tequila is unaged, clear, and has a pure agave flavor. Reposado tequila is aged in oak barrels for at least 2 months, giving it a smoother taste and hints of oak. Joven tequila is a blend of blanco and reposado, creating a unique flavor profile. Anejo tequila is aged for at least one year in oak barrels, resulting in a rich and complex flavor. Other types may include extra anejo or flavored tequilas, offering a diverse range of options for consumers looking for premium quality.</p></p>
