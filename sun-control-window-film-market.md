<p><ul><li>Clear (Non-Reflective)</li><li>Dyed (Non-Reflective)</li><li>Vacuum Coated (Reflective)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sun control window film is divided into three main types: Clear (Non-Reflective), Dyed (Non-Reflective), and Vacuum Coated (Reflective). Clear films are designed to offer protection without altering the appearance of the window. Dyed films provide additional sunlight filtering and privacy. Vacuum coated films feature a metallic layer that reflects sunlight, keeping interiors cool and reducing glare. Each type of window film offers unique benefits and caters to different preferences and needs in terms of sun control and aesthetics.</p></p>
