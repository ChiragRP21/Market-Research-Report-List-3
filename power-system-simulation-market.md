There are various types of power system simulation markets, including arc flash simulation, short circuit simulation, and equipment coordination selective simulation. Arc flash simulation involves assessing the risk of electrical arcs and explosions in power systems. Short circuit simulation analyzes the effects of short circuits on power system components. Equipment coordination selective simulation focuses on optimizing the coordination of protection devices in power systems. Other markets in power system simulation may include load flow simulation, voltage stability simulation, and dynamic stability simulation.</p></p>
