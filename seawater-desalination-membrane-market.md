<p><p>Seawater desalination membranes are crucial components in the process of converting seawater into fresh water. The market primarily consists of three types of membranes: Electrodialysis Membrane, Reverse Osmosis Membrane, and Other membranes. Electrodialysis membranes work by selectively passing ions through membranes, while Reverse Osmosis membranes use pressure to remove salt from seawater. Other membranes may include nanofiltration or ultrafiltration membranes. Each type of membrane plays a unique role in the desalination process, catering to different requirements and preferences of customers.</p></p>
