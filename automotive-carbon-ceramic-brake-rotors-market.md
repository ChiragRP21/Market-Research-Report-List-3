<p><p>The automotive carbon ceramic brake rotors market consists of three main types: drilled rotors, slotted rotors, and other types. Drilled rotors have strategically placed holes across the surface, which help dissipate heat and improve brake performance. Slotted rotors have narrow grooves, allowing gas, water, and debris to escape, enhancing braking efficiency. The "other" category includes alternative rotor designs that may feature combinations of drilled holes and slots or unique patterns to offer improved braking capabilities. These different rotor types aim to enhance the overall braking performance and safety of vehicles.</p></p>
