<p><p>The Adaptor Subs market consists of various types catering to different drilling operations. Water Swivel Adapter Subs are used to connect the water swivel to the drill string, enabling the circulation of drilling fluid. Hoisting Adapter Subs are designed to facilitate the hoisting of heavy equipment during drilling operations. Casing Adapter Subs are used to connect casing strings of different sizes. Recovery Tool Adapter Subs are utilized to retrieve stuck tools or equipment from the wellbore. Each type serves a specific function in the drilling process.</p></p>
