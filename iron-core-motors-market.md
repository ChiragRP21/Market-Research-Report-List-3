<p><strong>The Iron Core Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Asynchronous</li><li>Synchronous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Iron core motors are commonly classified into two types based on their operating principles: asynchronous and synchronous motors. In asynchronous motors, the rotor rotates at a slightly slower speed than the rotating magnetic field in the stator, creating the necessary torque for operation. On the other hand, synchronous motors operate at a constant speed that is synchronized with the frequency of the electrical supply. Both types of iron core motors are widely used in various industrial and commercial applications for their respective advantages and performance characteristics.</p></p>
