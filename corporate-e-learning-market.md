<p><p>The corporate e-learning market encompasses two main types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise e-learning refers to a system that is installed and managed locally within an organization's infrastructure. It offers more control over data security and customization but requires higher upfront costs and maintenance. On the other hand, cloud-based e-learning operates over the internet, allowing for easy access and scalability. It offers flexibility, reduced infrastructure costs, and automatic updates. However, there may be concerns regarding data privacy and reliance on internet connectivity.</p></p>
