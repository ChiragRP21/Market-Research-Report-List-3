<p><p>PU Foam Stabilizers are essential additives used in the production of various types of polyurethane foams. For flexible urethane foams, stabilizers help maintain the foam's softness and flexibility. For cold-cure foams, stabilizers assist in the curing process at lower temperatures. For semi-rigid urethane foams, stabilizers provide the right balance of rigidity and flexibility. Each type of stabilizer is specifically formulated to meet the unique requirements of the different foam applications, ensuring high-quality and reliable foam production.</p></p>
