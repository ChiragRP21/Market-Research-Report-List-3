<p><p>Waveguide attenuators are devices that reduce the power of a signal without affecting the waveform. Different types of waveguide attenuators include Calibrated Attenuators which provide accurate and precise attenuation levels, Direct Reading Attenuators which show the exact attenuation value on a scale, Fixed Attenuators with a set attenuation value, Variable Attenuators that allow for adjustable attenuation levels, and other specialized attenuators designed for specific applications such as step attenuators or programmable attenuators. Each type serves a unique purpose in RF and microwave systems.</p></p>
