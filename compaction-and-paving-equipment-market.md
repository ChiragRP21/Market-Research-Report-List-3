<p><p>Compaction and paving equipment market includes various types such as compaction rollers, jumping jacks/rammers, pavers, plate compactors, and scrapers. Compaction rollers are used for flattening and compacting surfaces. Jumping jacks/rammers are ideal for compacting narrow areas. Pavers are used for laying asphalt or concrete on roads. Plate compactors are used for compacting granular soils. Scrapers are used for leveling and smoothing surfaces. Each type of equipment serves a specific purpose in the construction industry to ensure smooth and durable surfaces.</p></p>
