<p><p>ICs for Wireless Charging System market includes transmitter ICs and receiver ICs. Transmitter ICs are responsible for converting electrical power into a wireless power signal and transmitting it to the receiver ICs. Receiver ICs, on the other hand, receive the wireless power signal and convert it back into electrical power to charge the device. Both transmitter and receiver ICs play a crucial role in enabling wireless charging technology to function efficiently and wirelessly charge devices.</p></p>
