<p><p>Capacitive position sensors are commonly categorized into two main types: linear position sensors and angular position sensors. Linear position sensors are used to measure linear displacement or movement along a straight path, while angular position sensors are used to measure rotational movement or angle changes. Both types of sensors utilize capacitance changes to detect the position of an object, providing high accuracy and reliability in various industrial and automotive applications. The market for these sensors is expected to grow due to increasing demand for precise motion control and automation technologies.</p></p>
