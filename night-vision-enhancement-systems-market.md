NIR systems use light sources that emit near-infrared wavelengths to enhance visibility in low-light conditions. FIR systems, on the other hand, detect the heat emitted by objects to create an image, making them effective in total darkness. Both types of systems have their own advantages and are used in various applications such as military, surveillance, and automotive industries.</p></p>
