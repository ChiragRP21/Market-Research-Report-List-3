<p><strong>The Carbon Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastic Composites (CFRTP) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyacrylonitrile (PAN)-based CFRTP</li><li>Pitch-based CFRTP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CFRTP is a type of composite material that combines carbon fiber with a thermoplastic polymer matrix. There are two main types of CFRTP: PAN-based and Pitch-based. PAN-based CFRTP is produced using polyacrylonitrile fibers, which offer high strength and stiffness. Pitch-based CFRTP is made from coal tar or petroleum pitch, known for its high thermal conductivity and resistance to chemicals. Both types of CFRTP are lightweight, durable, and widely used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and sports equipment.</p></p>
