<p><p>Inertial systems for aerospace are used for navigation and control in aircraft and spacecraft. AHRS type systems combine data from accelerometers, gyroscopes, and magnetometers to provide attitude and heading information. INS type systems use accelerometers and gyroscopes to track movement and position. IMU type systems consist of a standalone unit with accelerometers and gyroscopes. Laser type systems measure distance using laser beams. Other types of inertial systems include GPS-aided systems and hybrid systems combining multiple technologies for improved accuracy and reliability in aerospace applications.</p></p>
