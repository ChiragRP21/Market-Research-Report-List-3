<p><p>Servo gearbox market types can be categorized into three main types: inline, right angle, and others. Right angle gearboxes are used in applications where space is limited or where a change in direction is needed. The "others" category includes unique gearboxes designed for specific applications that don't fit into the inline or right angle categories. Each type of gearbox offers different advantages and is chosen based on the specific needs of the application.</p></p>
