<p><p>Automotive Super Swamper Tires come in two main market types, Bias Tire and Radial Tire. Bias tires are made with crisscrossed layers of fabric, providing a stiffer sidewall and greater resistance to punctures, making them suitable for off-road terrain. On the other hand, Radial tires have layers of cords across the tire, running at right angles to the direction of travel, offering a smoother ride and better fuel efficiency on paved roads. Both types cater to different driving conditions and preferences.</p></p>
