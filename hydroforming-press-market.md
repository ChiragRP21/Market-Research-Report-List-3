<p><p>Hydroforming presses come in different types based on their operation method. Computer Numerical Control (CNC) hydroforming presses are automated and use computer programs to control the pressure and movement of the press. Mechanical hydroforming presses, on the other hand, rely on manual operation and physical force to shape the metal. Both types have their own advantages and are suited for different applications based on the level of precision and control needed in the metal forming process.</p></p>
