<p><p>Polycarbonate film for optical market can be categorized into two main types: Optically Isotropic Film and Optically Anisotropic Film. Optically Isotropic Film provides uniform optical properties in all directions, making it suitable for applications where consistent light transmission is required. On the other hand, Optically Anisotropic Film exhibits varying optical properties depending on the direction of light, offering unique visual effects and functionalities. Both types cater to different needs in the optical industry, providing versatility and innovation in various applications.</p></p>
