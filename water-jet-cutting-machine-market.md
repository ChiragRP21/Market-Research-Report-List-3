<p><ul><li>Pressurized Water</li><li>Abrasive Mixture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water jet cutting machines can be categorized into two main types: Pressurized water and abrasive mixture market. Pressurized water cutting machines use high-pressure water to cut materials like plastics, rubber, and foam. On the other hand, abrasive mixture cutting machines combine water with abrasive particles like garnet to cut harder materials like metal, stone, and glass. Both types offer precise cutting capabilities and are widely used in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
