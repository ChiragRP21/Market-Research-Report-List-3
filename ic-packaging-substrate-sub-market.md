<p><p>IC Packaging Substrate market includes three main types: Organic, Inorganic, and Composite substrates. Organic substrates, made from materials like epoxy resins, are cost-effective and suitable for low to mid-range applications. Inorganic substrates, usually made of ceramics or glass, offer better thermal conductivity and stability for high-performance applications. Composite substrates combine the strengths of both organic and inorganic materials, providing a balance between cost-effectiveness and performance. Each type of substrate caters to different requirements in the IC packaging industry.</p></p>
