<p><p>Highly dispersible silica is available in two main forms on the market - granular and micropearl. Granular silica is typically larger in size and has a higher density, making it more suitable for applications where flow properties are important. On the other hand, micropearl silica is smaller in size and has a lower density, providing improved dispersion and compatibility with other materials. Both forms offer unique benefits and are used in various industries such as rubber, plastics, coatings, and advanced materials.</p></p>
