<p><p>Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Filters are categorized into Transversal Filters and Resonator Filters in the market. Transversal Filters utilize interdigital transducers for signal processing, offering high performance in narrowband applications. Resonator Filters, on the other hand, rely on acoustic resonators to achieve precise frequency control and high selectivity in wideband applications. Both types of SAW Filters play a crucial role in various wireless communication devices, automotive systems, and other electronic applications, catering to the diverse needs of the market.</p></p>
