<p><p>Metal Core PCB Market is categorized into three types based on the material used in the core: Aluminum Core PCB, Copper Core PCB, and Alloys Core PCB. Aluminum Core PCBs provide excellent thermal conductivity and are suitable for applications requiring high heat dissipation. Copper Core PCBs offer higher thermal conductivity than aluminum cores, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. Alloys Core PCBs combine different metals to achieve a balance between thermal conductivity and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for a wide range of applications.</p></p>
