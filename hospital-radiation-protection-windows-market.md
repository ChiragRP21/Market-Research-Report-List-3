<p><p>The Hospital Radiation Protection Windows market offers three types of windows: fixed, slide, and telescopic. Fixed windows are stationary and don't move, providing a permanent barrier against radiation. Slide windows can be opened horizontally or vertically, allowing for ventilation without compromising radiation protection. Telescopic windows feature movable panels that stack behind one another, enabling a larger opening when needed. These different window types cater to hospitals' diverse needs for radiation protection while providing flexibility and convenience in terms of functionality.</p></p>
