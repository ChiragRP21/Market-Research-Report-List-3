<p><p>The Concrete Sound Barrier Market consists of two main types: Ground-Mounted and Structure-Mounted barriers. Ground-Mounted barriers are installed directly into the ground and are typically used in outdoor settings to block or reduce noise from highways, railways, and industrial areas. Structure-Mounted barriers, on the other hand, are attached to existing structures like buildings or bridges to provide noise insulation and absorption. Both types of barriers play a crucial role in reducing noise pollution and creating quieter environments for residents and workers.</p></p>
