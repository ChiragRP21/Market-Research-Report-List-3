<p><p>Clinical data analytics refers to the use of technology and statistical methods to analyze large sets of clinical data in order to gain meaningful insights for healthcare decision-making. There are two main types of market for clinical data analytics: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions involve installing and managing the analytics software within the healthcare organization's own infrastructure. In contrast, cloud-based solutions are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet.
