<p><strong>The Separated Metal Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Induction</li><li>X-ray</li><li>Microwave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electromagnetic induction metal detectors use a coil to generate a magnetic field, which induces currents in metallic objects, allowing them to be detected. X-ray metal detectors use X-ray technology to penetrate materials and identify the presence of metal. Microwave metal detectors use microwave technology to detect metal objects based on changes in the signal caused by the presence of metal. These three types of metal detectors are commonly used in various industries for security screening, quality control, and material inspection purposes.</p></p>
