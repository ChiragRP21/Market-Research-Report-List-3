<p><p>Methylene Diphenyl Isocyanate (MDI) is a key chemical used in the production of polyurethane foams, coatings, adhesives, and sealants. There are two main market types for MDI: Pure MDI and Polymeric MDI. </p><p>Pure MDI is a high-quality, monomeric form of MDI that is used in applications requiring high performance and purity, such as medical devices and specialty coatings. </p><p>Polymeric MDI, on the other hand, is a blend of MDI isomers that is used in the production of rigid and flexible polyurethane foams for insulation, furniture, and automotive applications.</p></p>
