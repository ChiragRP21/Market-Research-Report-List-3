<p><p>Orthopedic Surgery Navigation Software Market Types:</p><p>1. Electromagnetic Navigation Systems: Utilize electromagnetic tracking sensors to provide real-time imaging and spatial information during surgery.</p><p>2. Hybrid Navigation Systems: Combine multiple navigation technologies, such as electromagnetic and optical systems, to enhance accuracy and visualization.</p><p>3. Optical Navigation Systems: Use cameras and optical markers to create 3D models of the patient's anatomy, allowing precise surgical guidance.</p><p>4. Fluoroscopy-Based Navigation Systems: Employ continuous X-ray imaging to track surgical instruments and implants, assisting in accurate placement.</p><p>5. CT-Based Navigation Systems: Utilize preoperative CT scans to generate a 3D virtual model, aiding surgeons in planning and performing complex orthopedic procedures.</p></p>
