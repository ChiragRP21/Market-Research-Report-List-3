<p><ul><li>Rigid First Aid Splint</li><li>Semi-Rigid First Aid Splint</li><li>Malleable First Aid Splint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The First Aid Splint market consists of three main types: Rigid, Semi-Rigid, and Malleable. Rigid First Aid Splints are made of materials that do not bend or flex, providing strong support and immobilization for injuries. Semi-Rigid splints offer both support and some flexibility, allowing for slight movement while still stabilizing the injured area. Malleable splints are highly flexible and moldable to fit the shape of a specific injury, providing customized support. Each type serves a different purpose and caters to different injury scenarios, ensuring proper immobilization and aid in the recovery process.</p></p>
