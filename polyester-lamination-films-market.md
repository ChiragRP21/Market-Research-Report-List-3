<p><p>Polyester lamination films are categorized based on their thickness into four main types: Up to 0.1 mm, 0.1-0.5 mm, 0.5-1 mm, and Above 1 mm. Films up to 0.1 mm are commonly used for lightweight packaging applications, while films ranging from 0.1-0.5 mm are suitable for medium-duty lamination purposes. Films in the 0.5-1 mm range are ideal for heavy-duty lamination applications, and those above 1 mm are typically utilized for specialized industrial uses that require extreme durability and protection.</p></p>
