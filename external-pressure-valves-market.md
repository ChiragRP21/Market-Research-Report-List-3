<p><p>External pressure valves are classified into two main types in the market - Type I and Type II. Type I valves are designed to release excess pressure in a gradual and controlled manner, ensuring safety and preventing sudden bursts or leaks. On the other hand, Type II valves are built to withstand higher levels of pressure and provide continuous protection even in extreme conditions. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining the integrity and efficiency of pressure systems in various industries.</p></p>
