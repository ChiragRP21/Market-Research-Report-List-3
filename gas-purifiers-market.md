<p><p>Gas purifiers are used to remove impurities from gases in various industries. There are two main types of gas purifiers: Point of Use Gas Purifiers and Bulk Gas Purifiers. </p><p>Point of Use Gas Purifiers are smaller units that are placed directly at the point where the gas is being used to ensure a clean supply. On the other hand, Bulk Gas Purifiers are larger systems used to purify gases in bulk before distribution to multiple points of use. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and purity of gases in industrial processes.</p></p>
