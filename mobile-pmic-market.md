<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Mobile PMIC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CMOS</li><li>BiCMOS</li><li>BCD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mobile PMIC (Power Management IC) market can be categorized into three main types based on the technology used - CMOS, BiCMOS, and BCD. CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) technology offers low power consumption and high integration, making it suitable for portable devices. BiCMOS (Bipolar CMOS) combines the benefits of both bipolar and CMOS technologies, providing high speed and low power dissipation. BCD (Bipolar-CMOS-DMOS) integrates bipolar, CMOS, and DMOS technologies, offering a balance between power handling capability and low power consumption for mobile applications.</p></p>
