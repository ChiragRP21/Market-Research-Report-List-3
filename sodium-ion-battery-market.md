<p><p>Sodium-ion Battery Market consists of three main types: Sodium-Sulfur Batteries, Sodium-Salt Batteries (Zebra Batteries), and Sodium-Oxygen (Sodium Air) Batteries. Sodium-Sulfur Batteries are known for their high energy density and long cycle life, making them ideal for grid storage applications. Sodium-Salt Batteries, or Zebra Batteries, are characterized by their high operating temperature and fast charging capabilities, making them suitable for electric vehicles. Sodium-Oxygen Batteries utilize oxygen from the air, offering a high theoretical energy density but facing challenges in practical implementation.</p></p>
