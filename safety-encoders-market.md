<p><p>Safety encoders are essential components used in various industrial applications to ensure the safe operation of machinery. There are two main types of safety encoders: Incremental Encoder and Absolute Encoder. Incremental encoders measure motion based on changes in position, providing relative feedback. Absolute encoders, on the other hand, provide precise position data with unique binary codes for each position. Both types of safety encoders are crucial for ensuring operator safety and preventing accidents in industrial settings.</p></p>
