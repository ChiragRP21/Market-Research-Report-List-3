<p><p>Laser patterning machines are classified into three main types: YAG laser type, CO2 laser type, and others. YAG laser type machines use solid-state technology and are typically used for cutting and engraving metals. CO2 laser type machines are widely used for cutting and engraving non-metallic materials such as wood, plastic, and glass. Other types of laser patterning machines include fiber lasers and UV lasers which are used for specialized applications like circuit board manufacturing. Each type offers unique capabilities and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
