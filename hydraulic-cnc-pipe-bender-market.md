<p><ul><li>Single-Head</li><li>Twin-Head</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hydraulic CNC Pipe Bender market consists of two main types - Single-Head and Twin-Head. Single-Head machines have one bending head that can bend pipes one at a time, making them suitable for smaller-scale operations and simpler bending requirements. Twin-Head machines, on the other hand, have two bending heads that can bend multiple pipes simultaneously, making them more efficient for larger-scale operations and complex bending tasks. Each type offers unique advantages based on the specific needs and scale of the project.</p></p>
