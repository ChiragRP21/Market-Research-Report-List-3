<p><strong>The Polymer Cement Waterproof Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I (High Elongation, High Poly-ash Ratio)</li><li>Type II (Low Elongation, Low Poly-ash Ratio)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polymer cement waterproof coating Market Types include Type I and Type II. Type I coatings have high elongation and a high poly-ash ratio, making them flexible and durable. They are suitable for applications where movement is expected. Type II coatings have low elongation and a low poly-ash ratio, providing a more rigid and less flexible option. These coatings are ideal for areas where little to no movement is anticipated. Both types offer different benefits depending on the specific needs of the project.</p></p>
