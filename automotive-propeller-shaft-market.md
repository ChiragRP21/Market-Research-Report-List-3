<p><p>The Automotive Propeller Shaft market consists of two main types: Single Propeller Shaft and Multi Piece Propeller Shaft. </p><p>Single Propeller Shaft is a solid shaft that connects the transmission to the differential in a vehicle, providing a single continuous link between the two components. </p><p>Multi Piece Propeller Shaft, on the other hand, consists of multiple segments joined together by universal joints. This type of propeller shaft allows for greater flexibility in the drivetrain and can accommodate larger vehicles or vehicles with unique powertrain configurations.</p></p>
