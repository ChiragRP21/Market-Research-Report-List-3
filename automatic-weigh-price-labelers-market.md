<p><p>Automatic Weigh Price Labelers are divided into two main market types: Semi-automatic and Fully-automatic. Semi-automatic machines require some manual input such as placing the product on the scale and pressing a button to print the label. Fully-automatic machines operate without much human intervention, as they can weigh the product, calculate the price, and print the label automatically. Both types offer efficiency and accuracy in weighing and labeling products, but the level of automation varies between the two.</p></p>
