<p><p>Membership management systems are software solutions that help organizations manage their membership processes and activities. There are two main market types for these systems: on-premises and cloud-based. </p><p>On-premises systems are installed and hosted on the organization's own servers, giving them complete control and security over their data. However, they require substantial upfront investments and ongoing maintenance.</p><p>In contrast, cloud-based systems are hosted on remote servers, allowing organizations to access their membership data anytime and anywhere through the internet.
