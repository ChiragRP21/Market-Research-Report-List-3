<p><strong>The Carbonless Continuous Paper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Multi-layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carbonless continuous paper is available in two main types: single layer and multi-layer. Single layer paper consists of a top sheet coated with micro-encapsulated dye and a bottom sheet coated with clay, which creates a copy when pressure is applied. Multi-layer paper has additional intermediate sheets with alternating coatings, allowing for multiple copies to be made simultaneously. Single layer paper is often used for simple forms and receipts, while multi-layer paper is typically used for more complex documents requiring multiple copies.</p></p>
