<p><strong>The Industrial Vacuum Sealer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nozzle Vacuum Sealer</li><li>Chamber Vacuum Sealer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial vacuum sealer market consists of two main types: the nozzle vacuum sealer and the chamber vacuum sealer. The nozzle vacuum sealer is a more portable option, suitable for smaller packaging requirements. It uses a nozzle to remove the air from the package before sealing it. On the other hand, the chamber vacuum sealer is larger and more suited for high-volume packaging. It operates by placing the entire package inside a chamber, where the air is removed and the sealing process takes place.</p></p>
