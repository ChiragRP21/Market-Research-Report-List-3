<p><ul><li>Single Channel Dash Cameras</li><li>Multi-Channel Dash Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single channel dash cameras are designed to record video footage from one perspective, typically facing forward. These devices are suitable for capturing events happening in front of the vehicle. On the other hand, multi-channel dash cameras feature multiple cameras that can record footage from various angles simultaneously, providing a more comprehensive view of the surroundings. They are ideal for capturing a wider area around the vehicle and provide more detailed and accurate information in case of an incident.</p></p>
