<p><p>Flexible AC Transmission (FACT) Systems market includes various types such as Shunt Compensation, Series Compensation, and Others. Shunt Compensation involves connecting devices such as capacitors and reactors in parallel with the transmission system to regulate voltage and improve system stability. Series Compensation involves connecting devices such as capacitors and reactors in series with the transmission line to enhance power transfer capacity and reduce line impedance. Other types in the market may include Unified Power Flow Controllers (UPFC) and Static VAR Compensators (SVC) for advanced grid control and operation.</p></p>
