<p><p>The genuine leather market consists of two main types: top-grain leather and split leather. Top-grain leather is the highest quality and most expensive type of leather, known for its durability and smooth finish. It is taken from the top layer of the hide, making it strong and long-lasting. Split leather, on the other hand, is taken from the lower layers of the hide and is more affordable and less durable than top-grain leather. It is often used in less expensive leather products.</p></p>
