<p><p>Monolithic refractories are a versatile type of refractory material used in various industries such as steel, cement, and glass. They are categorized into different types based on their applications - Castables, which are mixtures of refractory aggregates and binders that can be poured or cast into place; Ramming Masses, which are granular materials that are compacted into place by hand or pneumatic tools; and other market types that include gunning mixes, plastic refractories, and mortars. Each type offers specific properties and advantages depending on the requirements of the application.</p></p>
