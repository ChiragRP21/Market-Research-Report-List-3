<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Temperature-Controlled Containers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Control</li><li>Passive Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pharmaceutical temperature-controlled containers market can be categorized into active control and passive control types. Active control containers are equipped with an external power source to regulate temperature continuously during transit. On the other hand, passive control containers rely on insulated materials and phase change materials to maintain temperature for a limited period. Active control containers are more expensive but provide precise temperature control, while passive control containers are more cost-effective but have limited temperature control capabilities.</p></p>
