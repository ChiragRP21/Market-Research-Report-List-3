<p><p>Vacuum quenching furnaces are a key equipment in the heat treatment industry, offering different types of quenching methods. Gas quenching involves using gases like nitrogen or helium to rapidly cool the material, resulting in a uniform and controlled quenching process. On the other hand, liquid quenching involves immersing the material in a liquid, such as oil or water, to cool it down quickly. Both gas and liquid quenching methods have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the material being processed.</p></p>
