<p><p>Horizontal Bagging Machines are available in two main types - Fully Automatic and Semi-automatic. Fully Automatic machines require minimal human intervention, as they can perform the entire bagging process from start to finish autonomously. On the other hand, Semi-automatic machines require some level of human input, such as placing the product in the bag or sealing the filled bag. Both types cater to different market needs, with Fully Automatic machines being ideal for high volume production and Semi-automatic machines being suitable for smaller scale operations.</p></p>
