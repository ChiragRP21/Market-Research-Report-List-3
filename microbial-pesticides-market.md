<p><p>Microbial pesticides market includes various types such as microbial insecticides, microbial fungicides, microbial herbicides, and others. Microbial insecticides are derived from bacteria, viruses, or fungi and are specifically targeted towards insect pests. Microbial fungicides are effective against fungal plant diseases. Microbial herbicides are derived from microorganisms that suppress or kill weeds. Other types of microbial pesticides include nematicides and molluscicides, which target nematodes and mollusks, respectively. Overall, these microbial pesticides offer environmentally friendly and sustainable alternatives to traditional chemical pesticides.</p></p>
