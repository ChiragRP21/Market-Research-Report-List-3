<p><strong>The Variable Reluctance Stepper Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Stack Variable Reluctance Motor</li><li>Multi Stack Variable Reluctance Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Variable reluctance stepper motors come in two main types: single stack and multi stack. Single stack variable reluctance motors have a simpler design with a single rotor and stator arrangement, making them more suitable for low torque applications. On the other hand, multi stack variable reluctance motors have multiple rotor-stator pairs stacked together, providing higher torque output and better performance for more demanding applications. Both types cater to different market needs, ranging from simple, light-duty tasks to complex, heavy-duty industrial applications.</p></p>
