<p><p>Saturated polyester resin is available in two main types: liquid and solid. Liquid saturated polyester resin is a viscous liquid that can be easily mixed with other materials to create various products such as paints, coatings, and adhesives. On the other hand, solid saturated polyester resin is in a solid form and requires heat to melt before it can be used in manufacturing processes. Both types of saturated polyester resin offer unique properties and applications in different industries such as automotive, construction, and electronics.</p></p>
