<p><strong>The Acrylic Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extruded Acrylic</li><li>Cast Acrylic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Extruded acrylic products are made by pushing melted acrylic through a die, resulting in a less expensive option with lower quality compared to cast acrylic products. On the other hand, cast acrylic products are made by pouring liquid acrylic into molds and allowing it to harden, creating a higher quality, more durable product. While extruded acrylic is suitable for basic applications, cast acrylic is preferred for higher-end applications that require clarity, scratch resistance, and better durability.</p></p>
