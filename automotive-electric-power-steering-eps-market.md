<p><strong>The Automotive Electric Power Steering (EPS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EPS</li><li>P-EPS</li><li>R-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Electric Power Steering (EPS) systems come in three main types: Column-EPS (C-EPS), Pinion-EPS (P-EPS), and Rack-EPS (R-EPS). C-EPS uses an electric motor mounted on the steering column, P-EPS has the motor directly attached to the steering gear, and R-EPS integrates the motor into the steering rack. Each type offers its own advantages in terms of performance, efficiency, and packaging. C-EPS provides a simpler design, P-EPS offers better energy efficiency, and R-EPS delivers improved responsiveness.</p></p>
