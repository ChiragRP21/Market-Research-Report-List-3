<p><p>The wire pin driver market includes two main types: cord driver and cordless driver. Cord drivers are the traditional wire pin drivers that rely on a power cord to function. They require a direct power source, which limits mobility but ensures a continuous power supply. On the other hand, cordless drivers are wire pin drivers that operate using rechargeable batteries. These drivers provide more flexibility and mobility as they are not restricted by a power cord. However, their battery life may be a limitation, requiring periodic recharging.</p></p>
