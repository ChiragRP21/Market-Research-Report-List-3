Automatic labeling machines, on the other hand, are suitable for larger production lines as they can automatically feed, label, and dispense products without human intervention. These machines are efficient, accurate, and save time and labor costs. Both types of machines play crucial roles in industries such as packaging, food and beverage, pharmaceutical, and cosmetics.</p></p>
