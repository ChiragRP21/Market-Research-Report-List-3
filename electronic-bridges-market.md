<p><p>Electronic bridges are devices that are used to measure electrical resistance, capacitance, and inductance in electrical circuits. There are different types of electronic bridges in the market. Network bridges are used to measure resistance and impedance in circuits. H circuit bridges are used to measure inductance. Wheatstone circuit bridges are used to measure resistance accurately. Other types of electronic bridges include Maxwell Bridges and Schering Bridges, which are used for specific measurement tasks. Each type of bridge serves a specific purpose in electronic measurements.</p></p>
